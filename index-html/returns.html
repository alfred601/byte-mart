Returns & Exchange Policy

A simple guide to returning, exchanging, or claiming warranty for your purchase.

At **ByteMart+**, we are committed to providing you with high-quality devices and accessories. We offer a **7-day return and exchange window** from the date your order is delivered, subject to the conditions below.

Return Eligibility and Process

What are the conditions for an eligible return?

To be eligible for a return or exchange, the item must meet all of the following criteria:

  • The product must be in the **same condition** that you received it (unused, unmarked, and undamaged).
  • The product must be in its **original, unopened packaging** with all seals intact.
  • All original accessories, manuals, and promotional materials must be included.
  • The return must be initiated and **authorized** by our support team within 7 days of delivery.

Returns that do not meet these criteria may be refused or subject to a restocking fee.

How do I start a return or exchange?

To initiate a return or exchange, you must contact our support team immediately (within the 7-day window) via WhatsApp or email with the following information:

  • **Order Number**
  • **Product Name and Model**
  • **Detailed Reason for Return** (e.g., Damaged on arrival, Wrong item sent, Change of mind)

Our team will provide you with a return authorization number and instructions on where to send the item. Do not send the item back without authorization.

Refunds, Exchanges, and Fees

What is the difference between Defective and Change of Mind returns?

Defective or Damaged Products

If your item arrives damaged or is found to be defective upon initial inspection (within 24 hours of delivery), we will arrange a **free exchange or a full refund**, including any standard shipping charges you paid. You must provide clear photographic or video evidence of the defect.

Returns Due to Change of Mind

Returns for a "change of mind" are accepted only if the item is **unopened and unused** (manufacturer seals must be intact). For this type of return, the customer is responsible for:

  • The cost of returning the item to our facility.
  • A potential restocking fee of up to 10% of the item's purchase price to cover administration and handling.
Which items are non-returnable?

The following items are generally **excluded** from our return policy, except in cases of confirmed manufacturing defect upon delivery:

  • Products that have been personalized, registered, or customized.
  • Items sold specifically as **"Final Sale"** or **"Clearance."**
  • Any product where the original **manufacturer's security seals have been broken or removed**.
  • Used screen protectors, worn accessories, or items involving hygiene (e.g., in-ear headphones).

Post-Return and Warranty

How long does a refund take?

Once your returned item is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed within **3-7 business days** to your original payment method (Mobile Money or Bank Account).

What is the warranty coverage for your products?

Our 7-day return policy covers initial defects and change-of-mind returns. Beyond this period, product issues are handled under the product's warranty:

  • **Phones:** All brand-new phones come with the **manufacturer's standard warranty** (typically 6-12 months).
  • **Accessories:** Accessories generally carry a limited 3-month replacement warranty against manufacturing defects.

We will assist you in initiating a warranty claim with the manufacturer, if necessary. Warranty terms will be specified on the product page.
