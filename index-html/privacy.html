Privacy Policy

Understanding how ByteMart+ collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

This Privacy Policy describes how ByteMart+ collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit or make a purchase from our site. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure shopping experience.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information Provided by You

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, place an order, or contact us. This includes:

  • **Identification Data:** Your name, email address, phone number, and physical delivery address.
  • **Transaction Data:** Details about the product you purchased and the payment method used (Note: We do not store full mobile money PINs or bank card numbers; secure processors handle payment details).
  • **Communication Data:** Information you provide when contacting customer support via email or WhatsApp.

    • This information is necessary to process your orders and communicate with you.

2. Automatic Data Collection (Usage and Technical Data)

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and interaction with our service, known as Usage Data. This may include:

  • **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • **Browsing Data:** Pages viewed, products searched, time spent on pages, and referring sites.

    • This data is used to analyze website performance, track trends, and improve the shopping experience.

How We Use Your Information

3. Purpose of Processing Your Data

We use the collected information for various legitimate business purposes, including:

  • **Order Fulfillment:** To process and deliver your goods (Name, address, contact).
  • **Communication:** To send you updates, tracking information, and respond to inquiries.
  • **Marketing:** To send promotional emails and special offers, but only if you have opted-in (you can opt-out anytime).
  • **Security and Fraud Prevention:** To verify transactions and prevent illegal activities, including compliance with our "Payment Before Shipment" policy.
4. Sharing and Disclosure of Data

We only share your information with third parties when necessary to run our business or comply with legal requirements:

  • **Service Providers:** We share necessary delivery details with our logistics partners (e.g., Courier services) for order delivery.
  • **Payment Processors:** Transaction data is shared with secure Mobile Money and bank payment gateways.
  • **Legal Compliance:** We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order).

    • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their independent marketing purposes.

Data Security and Rights

5. Data Security Measures

We implement robust technical and organizational measures designed to secure your personal information. This includes:

  • **Encryption:** Using SSL technology to protect data transmission on our site.
  • **Access Control:** Restricting employee access to your personal data to only those who require it for specific job functions.
  • **Payment Security:** Outsourcing payment processing to certified, secure payment platforms.

However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us:

Email: bytemartug@gmail.com

Cookie Policy

7. Use of Cookies

We use "cookies" to help us remember and process the items in your shopping cart, understand and save your preferences for future visits, and compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction. You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies through your browser settings.
