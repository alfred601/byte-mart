Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about ordering, delivery, and payments.

Ordering and Availability

Where can I see the products in person?

ByteMart+ operates **strictly online**. We do not maintain a physical retail location for product viewing, inspection, or pickup. All purchases and inquiries must be made through our website or contact channels.

Is the item I want currently available?

All products listed on the website show their real-time inventory status (e.g., "In Stock," "Low Stock," or "Out of Stock"). While we strive for 100% accuracy, stock levels can change quickly. Your order is only confirmed upon successful payment.

Do you accept bulk orders?

Yes, we do! For large or bulk purchases (5+ items), please contact our sales team directly at bytemartug@gmail.com for special pricing and delivery arrangements.

Payment Methods and Security

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of secure payment options:

  • **Mobile Money:** MTN MoMo and Airtel Money (Primary methods).
  • **Bank Transfer:** Direct bank deposit.
  • **Cryptocurrency:** Bitcoin (BTC) is accepted.

    • Note: All orders require **full payment confirmation before dispatch**.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?

Cash on Delivery (COD) is available only for select, established customers and specific low-value accessories within Kampala. For all high-value items (like phones), payment must be made in full and confirmed prior to delivery as a fraud prevention measure.

How secure is my payment information?

We use encrypted, verified payment processors for all transactions. We never store your full Mobile Money PIN or bank card details on our servers. Security is our top priority.

Shipping and Delivery

Where do you deliver?

We primarily focus on quick delivery within **Kampala** and the surrounding metropolitan area. We can arrange shipping to other regions of Uganda, but this will involve longer delivery times and higher fees.

How long does delivery take?

We offer a **Quick Ship Guarantee**:

  • **Kampala:** Orders paid before 1 PM are typically shipped the **same business day**.
  • **Outside Kampala:** Delivery usually takes 2-3 business days depending on the courier service and location.
Can I track my order?

Yes. Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number and a link to our logistics partner's tracking portal via email or WhatsApp.

Returns and Warranty

What is your return policy?

We accept returns or exchanges within **7 days** of delivery only for items with a confirmed manufacturing defect or if the wrong item was shipped. We do not accept returns for buyer's remorse, change of mind, or if the product has been used or damaged by the customer.

Do your products come with a warranty?

All brand new phones come with a standard manufacturer's warranty, which varies by brand (typically 6-12 months). Accessories generally have a limited 3-month warranty against manufacturing defects. Warranty terms will be specified on the product page.
