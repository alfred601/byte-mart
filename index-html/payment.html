Secure Payment Methods

Simple, Fast, and Secure ways to pay for your next gadget.

At ByteMart+, we strive to offer the most convenient and secure payment options for our customers across Uganda. Choose the method that best suits your needs during checkout.

Fully Encrypted Payments Fraud Protection Mobile Verified

Mobile Money Payments

1. MTN Mobile Money & Airtel Money

Mobile Money is the fastest and most widely accepted payment method in Uganda. Our system uses a secure gateway to instantly process payments from your mobile wallet.

MTN Mobile Money

Pay directly from your registered MTN Mobile Money number. A prompt will appear on your phone to confirm the transaction.

MTN MoMo

Airtel Money

Complete your purchase using your Airtel Money account. Follow the USSD prompts on your device to authorize the payment.

Airtel Money

How it works: At checkout, select your Mobile Money provider, enter your phone number, and a payment request will be sent to your device for immediate authorization.

Crypto Currency (Bitcoin)

2. Bitcoin Payments

For customers who prefer the security and speed of crypto payments, we accept **Bitcoin (BTC)**. This option is currently processed manually.

Bitcoin (BTC) Payments

Upon checkout, you will receive our Bitcoin wallet address and the exact BTC amount equivalent to your order total at the time of purchase.

**Note:** The conversion rate is locked at the time of order placement. Payment must be confirmed within 2 hours.

Bank Transfer

3. Direct Bank Transfer

We accept direct bank transfers for order payments. Please note that order processing will only begin once the transfer has been confirmed in our account, which may take **1-2 business days** depending on your bank.

Direct Bank Transfer Details

When selecting this option at checkout, our bank details will be provided. **Important:** You must use your **Order Number** as the reference to ensure your payment is successfully matched to your purchase.

Please confirm the total amount including shipping before initiating the transfer.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

4. Cash on Delivery (COD)

For orders within **Kampala and select surrounding areas**, we offer a Cash on Delivery option. This allows you to pay for your item in cash directly to our delivery agent upon receipt of the goods.

  • Availability: Limited to specific delivery zones. Please confirm eligibility during checkout.
  • Maximum Limit: COD may have an upper limit for large orders.
  • Note: Please have the exact amount ready as our delivery agents carry limited change.

Security and Policies

5. Security Guarantee

Your financial security is our top priority. We use secure, encrypted methods for payment processing. We do not store any sensitive financial information on the ByteMart+ website.

If you encounter any payment issues or have security concerns, please contact our support team immediately.
